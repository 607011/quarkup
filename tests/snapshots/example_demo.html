Mathematical and Chemical States

In Quarkup, the gravity of the text is incredibly easy to control.

A well-known molecule is H2O, while in mathematics we love to calculate with a2 + b2 = c2.

We can also easily emphasize text energetically, add visual mass to it, or declare it as inline_code.

Furthermore, we can harness the power of inline math. The energy is defined by or the wave function drifting directly within the flow of our text.

  • Full performance powered by our Rust parser

Tables a.k.a. Lattices

Complex tables are also possible. They can span columns and rows, and have multiple header lines, intermediate headers, and footers:

.l 
h: Product ; Properties ; > ; Mass
h: ; Charge ; Spin ;
s: Leptons
Electron ; .> −1 ; .> 1/2 ; .> 0.511 MeV
Neutrino ; .> 0 ; .> 1/2 ; .> < 0.12 eV
s: Quarks
Up quark ; .> +2/3 ; .> 1/2 ; .> 2.2 MeV
Down quark ; .> −1/3 ; .> 1/2 ; .> 4.7 MeV
.m Total mass.. ; > ; > ; .> .m ~ 7.5 MeV..
..

becomes

Product Properties Mass
Charge Spin
Leptons
Electron −1 1/2 0.511 MeV
Neutrino 0 1/2 < 0.12 eV
Quarks
Up quark +2/3 1/2 2.2 MeV
Down quark −1/3 1/2 4.7 MeV
Total mass ~ 7.5 MeV
